Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we handle your data.

Our Commitment

Cinetex is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe you should be in control of your data, which is why we've built a service that respects your privacy.

Local Storage Only

All your personal data — including watch history, favorites, ratings, and custom lists — is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. Your browsing behavior is not monitored, collected, or sold to third parties.

Third-Party APIs

Cinetex fetches movie metadata from The Movie Database (TMDB) API. These requests are made directly from your browser and are subject to TMDB's privacy policy. We do not store or process any data from these requests on our servers.

Data Control

You have complete control over your data. To remove all stored information, simply clear your browser's localStorage for this site. No data recovery is possible after deletion.
