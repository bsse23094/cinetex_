Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Below is a short summary of what Cinetex stores and why.

Data We Store

Cinetex stores minimal data locally in your browser: watch progress and lists. No server-side personal data storage is performed by default.

Local-only

Lists and progress are saved to localStorage on your device.

Third-party APIs

We request metadata from TMDB and other public APIs; those requests may be visible to those services.

Controls

Clear local storage in your browser to remove saved lists and progress.
